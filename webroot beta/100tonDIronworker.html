100 Ton Deluxe Ironworker

Ideal for the Large Shop

$22,496
  • Five Loaded Workstations
  • Built-in Rod Shear for Square and Round Bar
  • 18" Flat Bar Shear
  • Dedicated Coper Notcher
  • Electric Stroke Control
  • ANSI B11.5-1988R(02) Compliant
  • ETL/UL Approved Controls

A brute of an Ironworker, the Edwards 100 Ton Deluxe JAWS Ironworker features super-duty construction and a powerful 10hp motor. Standard features include a Punch Assembly, 18" wide Flat Bar Shear, Angle Shear, Coper Notcher and a special built-in Rod Shear for solid square and round bar stock.

The optional Hydraulic Accessory Pack allows your Ironworker to act as a power source to our expanding line of innovative Hydraulic Accessory Tools, such as the 10 Ton Tube/Pipe Bender, 20 Ton Shop Press and 40 Ton Shop Press.
