Designed for simultaneous use by two people, the Edwards ELITE 110|65 Ironworker is the most powerful of the new ELITE line. It features a standard Punch Assembly, 24" wide Flat Bar Shear, Angle Shear, a dedicated Coper Notcher and a universal open tooling station for optional Tooling Accessories.

The integrated Hydraulic Accessory Pack enables your ELITE 110|65 to act as a power source for our expanding line of innovative Hydraulic Accessory Tools.

Shown with optional Pipe Notcher tooling.
