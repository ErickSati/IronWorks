The Edwards ELITE 110 Ironworker sets the standard of design excellence and superior performance with integrated value-added features in this robust new series of Ironworkers.

Standard features of the ELITE 110 include a Punch Assembly, Flat Bar Shear, Angle Shear and a universal open tooling station for optional Tooling Accessories. Integrated hydraulic accessory ports enable your ELITE 110 to act as a power source for our expanding line of innovative Hydraulic Accessory Tools.

Shown with optional Coper Notcher tooling.
