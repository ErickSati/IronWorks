Powered by the 40Ton (A) through 120Ton (A) Ironworker models, Elite Ironworker Series or our Porta Power, the 10 Ton Tube / Pipe Bender is a quick-connect hydraulic tool that expands your shop without breaking the bank. Tube/Pipe Bender Dies      $137 - $695

Build your next project with pipe or tube!

Your Edwards 10 Ton Bender will bend solid round, schedule 40 pipe, tube and d.o.m. tubing when equipped with the proper, matched die sets and careful operation. Die sets are designed to center the bending work within the head-frame assembly. This design feature symmetrically distributes bending forces through the head-frame, eliminating asymmetrical stress, torque and premature wear of your machine.

Die sets include:

  • Forming Die
  • Saddle Strap
  • Saddle Bolt
  • Degree Pointer
  • Fine Adjustment Bolt Wiper Mounting Block
  • Wiper Blades (2)
  • Wiper Mounting Bolts (2) Adjustment Set-Screw
