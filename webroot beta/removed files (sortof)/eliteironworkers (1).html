Edwards Manufacturing has long enjoyed supporting the steel fabrication industry with a full line of high quality, low maintenance ironworking machines. With over 30,000 Edwards Ironworkers in service throughout the world, our expertise in Ironworker design and manufacturing along with inspiration from our valued Customers and Dealership have brought us to design a new generation of Ironworker.

The Edwards Elite Ironworker series takes all the best elements of our Jaws Ironworker products and improves upon them with integrated value-added features, superior performance and a fresh new look. Our Elite Ironworkers boast elegant design, engineering excellence and robust construction with new standard features including integrated LED lighting, hydraulic hold-downs, integrated measuring system, hydraulic accessory controls and quick-change punch station. Our state-of-the-art power unit has lock-out/tag-out and emergency palm stop features.

Take a look at the Edwards Elite Ironworkers and compare them to any high-end Ironworker in the marketplace and you'll see where we lead the pack. True to our history, our Elite Ironworkers provide extraordinary performance and incredible value for the dollar spent.
