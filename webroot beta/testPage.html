The Edwards ELITE 68 Ironworker offers all of the innovative features, superior performance and robust construction of the ELITE 110 and the ELITE 110|65. The distinguishing characteristic of the ELITE 68 can be found at the open station where unique features of a 9" open height and 2-1/16" stroke accommodate a huge range of tooling.

Standard features of the ELITE 68 include a Punch Assembly with Pedestal Die Table, Flat Bar Shear, Angle Shear, and a universal open tooling station for optional Tooling Accessories. Integrated hydraulic accessory ports enables your ELITE 68 to act as a power source for our expanding line of innovative Hydraulic Accessory Tools.
